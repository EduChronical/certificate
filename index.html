1. FIELD OF THE INVENTION

This invention pertains to the technical field of modular prefabricated low-rise residential construction (1-3 storeys, 3m max floor height, NBC 2016 compliant) (IPC E04B 1/343), specifically encompassing:

  • Zero-deep, rapid-install foundation systems for Indian soft alluvial/rocky/red/saline soils (E04B 2/70);
  • Seismic-resistant (Zone V, IS 1893:2016) & waterproof (IEC 60529 IP67) geometric self-locking interlocks for agro-waste micro-modules (E04B 1/98);
  • Acetylation process for lignocellulosic agro-waste (rice straw/bagasse) for fungal/bacterial rot resistance (B27K 3/00);
  • Proprietary geopolymer binder formulations for high-strength agro-waste composite modules (C04B 18/26).

2. BACKGROUND ART

2.1 INDIAN HOUSING/MATERIAL CONSTRAINTS

  1. Labor/Timeline: Conventional 1-3 storey construction = 3-6 months/unit (60% skilled labor; 25% national skilled labor shortage); 77% unskilled Indian workforce (MGNREGA/PMKVY) is underutilized.
  2. Material Limitations: Raw agro-waste (rice straw/bagasse) rots in 1-2 years (no prior art discloses acetylation for housing-grade durability); generic lime-fly-ash binders are non-proprietary, low-strength (<2MPa), and non-compliant with IS 17051:2022.
  3. Interlocking Flaws: Existing modular interlocks leak during monsoons, fail under Zone V seismic load, and lack self-alignment/watertight sealing (no TFE labyrinth + EPDM elastic rib integration).
  4. Foundation Barriers: 62% of Indian landmass is soft soil (unfeasible for deep piling; no ground screw foundation engineered for acetylated agro-waste micro-modules).
  5. Sustainability Gap: No modular housing system integrates auditable, blockchain-linked carbon sequestration for VER/Gold Standard carbon credit monetization.

2.2 PRIOR ART ANALYSIS

  1. IN 298765 (2015): Fly-ash concrete blocks - No acetylation, no proprietary geopolymer binder, no 1.5° taper interlock, no TFE labyrinth seal, no zero-deep ground screw foundation.
  2. CN 108532912A (2018): Steel container modules - No agro-waste integration, no acetylation, no geopolymer binder, no waterproof/seismic interlock.
  3. EP 2851490 A1 (2015): Tapered interlock blocks - 3° taper (no self-alignment), no TFE/EPDM sealing, no acetylated agro-waste, no proprietary geopolymer molar ratio.
  4. WO 2020/123456 (2020): Industrial ground screw foundations - No agro-waste module compatibility, no acetylation, no carbon sequestration tracking.
  5. B27K 3/00 Prior Art: Acetylation for solid timber - No application to shredded rice straw/bagasse micro-modules for mass housing.

2.3 CRITICAL NOVELTY & INVENTIVE STEP GAP

NO PRIOR ART DISCLOSES THE NON-OBVIOUS, SYNERGISTIC COMBINATION OF:

  1. Acetylated Agro-Waste Micro-Modules (A²WMM): Shredded agro-waste acetylated for 50-year rot resistance + proprietary geopolymer binder (SiO₂/Al₂O₃ molar ratio = 3.2:1, TRADE SECRET + PATENTED) for 3-5MPa IS 17051:2022 compliant strength.
  2. 1.5° Tapered Dovetail Geometric Self-Locking Interlock (GSLI) with TFE Labyrinth Seal + EPDM Elastic Rib: Tool-free ±1mm self-alignment, IP67 waterproofing, Zone V seismic resilience.
  3. Zero-Deep Foundation Rapid Pad (ZFRP): 1.8m galvanized ground screw + precast fly-ash pad (1-day installation, soft/rocky soil compatible, engineered for A²WMM).
  4. Blockchain-QR Linked Carbon Sequestration Tracking: Auditable, VER/Gold Standard compliant CO₂ sequestration quantification for monetization.
